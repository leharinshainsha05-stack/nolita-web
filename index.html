The Space

New York Soul,
Chennai Vibe.

Step into a slice of Lower Manhattan in the heart of Nungambakkam. Nolita’s space is a striking harmony of raw, industrial styling and vibrant, expressive design.

Vibrant Sky-Blue Ceiling

A bold, matte sky-blue ceiling grid with matching exposed piping creates an airy, energetic vibe.

Shattered Terrazzo Mosaic

Our custom black-and-white irregular mosaic tile flooring gives the space an artistic, hand-crafted edge.

Industrial Warmth

Warm ambient bulbs in low-hanging black pendant lights glow over dark walnut wooden tables.

Our Journey

NYC Roots. Chennai Soul.

Follow the timeline of how we brought the spirit of Lower Manhattan to Nungambakkam's premier culinary street.

2018
2018

The NYC Spark

Inspired by the espresso bars and rustic sourdough pizzerias of NoLiTa (North of Little Italy), Manhattan. We set out to bring the authentic 48-hour slow-fermentation craft to India.

Origin
2020
2020

Baking the Sourdough

Years of recipe testing perfected our 48-hour fermentation cycle. Stretched entirely by hand, baked at 450°C inside a wood-fired brick oven for the signature leopard-spotted crust char.

Craft
2023
2023

Chennai Launch

Nolita opens doors in Nungambakkam on Khader Nawaz Khan Road, Chennai. We introduced our signature sky-blue ceiling pipes, matte grids, and shattered terrazzo mosaic floors.

Milestone
2026
2026

Today & Beyond

We are Chennai's top-rated destination for sourdough pizza, craft burgers, and loaded milkshakes. A perfect gathering spot for coffee dates, work sessions, and family dinners.
